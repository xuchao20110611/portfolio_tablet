Introduction
The hunt tasks serves as the ice-breaking activities which includes several gamified challenges. We are group 15J, team hUmanX and the team members are Aditya Mankare, Angelica Tome, Chao Xu, Danni Xiao and Grace Song. In the hunt, our group work together to decode some instructions, do observations and make decision on our group name and potential project ranking lists.
