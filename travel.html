Introduction
In this page I will display some of tourist spots I have ever been.
Mount Song
Mountain Song is where Shaolin Temple is located. (Hover on the image to see more)
The ancient name of Mount Song , also known as Waifang , Songgao , and Sublime , is located in the central part of Henan Province and belongs to the Funiu Mountains . It is located in the northwest of Dengfeng City and is the Zhongyue of the Five Mountains .

Mount Wutai
Mountain Wutai is regarded as the home of Mañjuśrī, the Bodhisattva of wisdom. (Hover on the image to see more)
Wutai Mountain is the only Chinese Green Temple ( Chinese Buddhism ) Huang Temple ( Tibetan Buddhism ) Buddhist temple each other, so the Han Mongolian Tibetan and other peoples in this harmonious sharing.

Mount Putuo
Mountain Putuo is the awakening position of bodhisattva Guanyin. (Hover on the image to see more)
The Putuo Mountain Scenic Area is known as the Haitian Buddha Kingdom and the Holy Land of the South China Sea. It is in the first batch of national key scenic spots. The highest point is Foding Mountain , about 300 meters above sea level.

Santorini Island
Santorini island is famous for the incredible sunset. (Hover on the image to see more)
Santorini is regarded by many as the Greek island of their dreams, and countless beautiful pictures about the island.
